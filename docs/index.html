What is CFR?

Counterfactual Regret Minimization is a self-play algorithm that converges to a Nash Equilibrium — an unexploitable strategy where no player can improve by changing their approach alone.

How It Works

The bot repeatedly plays against itself, tracking regret for each decision. Over millions of iterations, it learns to minimize total regret, producing an optimal mixed strategy for every game state.

Play Modes

PvP — two humans on the same screen. PvB — test yourself against the trained bot. BvB — watch two bots play using their Nash Equilibrium strategies.

Leduc Poker

A step up in complexity. 6-card deck with a community card, 2 betting rounds, and raise/fold mechanics. Rich strategic depth.

Full 52-card deck with 4 betting rounds. Uses equity bucketing to abstract the massive game tree into a tractable model.

  • Actions: Fold, Check, Bet, Call, Raise
  • Buckets: 8 equity levels
